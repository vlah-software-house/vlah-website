## Overview

**Branding Refresh**: A complete branding package was developed, including logo design, comprehensive brand book, eye-catching banners, informational flyers, and engaging street advertising banners, tailored for the real estate market.

**Website and CRM System**: An intuitive, user-friendly website integrated with a custom CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system was constructed. This platform supports efficient management of properties, client interactions, and scheduling, enhancing operational efficiency and customer service.

**Portal Integration**: Seamless integration with Storia.ro and Imobiliare.ro, the leading real estate portals in Romania, allowed for broader outreach and streamlined property listing processes.

**SEO Optimization**: Local SEO strategies were implemented to boost visibility in regional search results, significantly increasing organic traffic and local engagement.

**Digital Advertising Campaigns**: Targeted advertising campaigns on Meta (Facebook) and Google were launched to reach potential clients effectively, enhancing brand recognition and lead generation.

**Sales Funnel and Chatbot Technology**: Manychat was utilized to create sophisticated sales funnels, integrating chatbots designed to capture and nurture leads through personalized, automated interactions.
